🔄 End-to-End Data Flow (Current State)

1) Mobile Sync Starts Pending local survey is posted to backend from SyncManager.
2) Survey + Photos Persist /api/surveys and /api/surveys/:id/photos complete with auth retry on 401.
3) Auto Complete Trigger Mobile now calls POST /api/surveys/:id/complete after successful sync.
4) Outbound Queue Enqueue Backend creates survey.completed event and stores in delivery queue.
5) Outbound Signed Delivery Worker signs payload using HMAC-SHA256(timestamp.rawBody) and posts to partner.
6) Inbound Receiver (SolarPro Side) Our new endpoint validates headers/signature and enforces idempotency by event_id.
7) Pre-Ingest Handshake Mode Returns 202 ACCEPTED_PRE_INGEST for valid signed payloads to avoid retry churn.
